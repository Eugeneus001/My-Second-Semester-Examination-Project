Why MealTracker?

MealTracker is a smart nutrition assistant that analyzes your food purchases using AI, no manual input required. By scanning receipts, restaurant orders, or digital transactions, it builds a personalized nutrition profile and offers clear, data-driven suggestions to help you eat better. Think of it as a dietitian powered by machine learning, working silently in the background.
